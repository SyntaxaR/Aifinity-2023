You search and we model for you to print

How to optain a 2D model in seconds?

Search and choose

First, enter what you want to look for as if you are using a search engine. Among the images offered to you, choose the one(s) that you would prefer.

Remove background and generate model

Our tool automatically removes background from the selected image(s) and generates a suitable 2D model based on the details in the selected image(s)

Print your model

Connect your device to a printer and click ‘Print’ to print the 2D model generated.
